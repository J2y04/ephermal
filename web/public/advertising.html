The Problem

Most brands waste 40% of their ad budget on guesswork.

Without data-driven allocation, you're flying blind across every platform you run ads on.

📊

No benchmarks → overspending

Without industry ROAS benchmarks, most merchants overbid on low-converting audiences and burn budget before learning what works.

⚖️

Equal allocation → wrong split

Splitting 50/50 between Meta and Google ignores your store's actual buyer journey. One channel almost always outperforms the other — AI knows which.

⏱️

Manual adjustments → missed scaling

By the time you notice a winning campaign and manually increase spend, the momentum window has closed. Automation catches it in hours, not days.

How It Works

Three steps. Then it runs itself.

From store connection to live campaigns in under 10 minutes.

1

Connect your store + ad accounts

Ephermal reads your product catalog, historical sales data, and existing campaign performance across Meta and Google. No manual data entry — it's all pulled automatically.

2

Set your revenue goal

Type in your monthly target. The AI reverse-engineers the daily budget, platform split, and ROAS threshold needed to hit it — calibrated to your store's real conversion data.

3

One-click launch

Campaigns go live immediately. The AI monitors performance hourly, reallocates budget from underperformers to winners, and pauses fatigued creatives before they drain spend.
